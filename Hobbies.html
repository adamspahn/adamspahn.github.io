Hobbies

Volley Ball

Volleyball has always been one of my all time favorite sports. Ever since I was in middle school, my friends would always play volleyball and I never understood what they found so fun in the sport. One time I tried giving it a chance and I found out that I was way better at it than I thought I would be. From then on I found out slowly growing on me and I started enjoying playing volleyball so much to the point where I joined the school's Volleyball team and played in tournaments against other schools in Bahrain. I would describe volleyball as one of my passions.

Ping Pong

Similar to volleyball, I initially did not have an interest in pingpong. But after I tried it, it became a very enjoyable sport to play with friends and family. One of the reasons why pingpong was a great sport for me to play with friends is because a few of my friends own pingpong tables and they have them inside their rooms. Whenever we are bored, we go to the pingpong table and just play against each other for hours.

Video Games

Ever since I was a kid, my dad got me a ps1 and I ended up buying games for it and those were the most enjoyable times of my childhood. I got to dive into the digital world of video games and enjoy so many different and unique storymode games that had amazing background stories and I fell in love with gaming consoles. I currently switched over to a gaming computer because I found that It was way more efficient for me to have one because I could also do school and university work on it, as well as go on online voice calls with my friends and play online video games with them.

Watching Shows

Since most people aren't born with a common heart disease, most people would have hobbies that are outdoor.
